What's it like to work at Yellow Pencil?

A job at Yellow Pencil means working at a web development company based in Edmonton, Alberta with an office in Vancouver, BC. We have been in business since 1996, and have a solid roster of long-term clients. We work with a variety of technologies, but always focus on content-driven, usable, accessible websites.

We are a committed shop with a strong regional and growing international client base. We are one of the top Open Text partners in North America. This exposes us to markets throughout the world, leading to big opportunities with big clients without having to leave Edmonton (much).

Working at Yellow Pencil is pretty awesome, especially if you like video games and plaid shirts. Sound like fun? Take a look at our current opportunities.
